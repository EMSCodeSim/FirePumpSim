FirePumpSim Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

FirePumpSim is a firefighter pump-operator training app designed for practice scenarios, pump pressure calculations, formulas, printable worksheets, and training reference tools.

Information We Collect

FirePumpSim does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

App Data

The app may store user settings, progress, pump card information, and training preferences locally on the user’s device. This information is used only to improve the app experience and is not sold or shared by FirePumpSim.

Purchases

Any app downloads, purchases, or subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. FirePumpSim does not receive or store payment card information.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Apple may provide standard App Store analytics, crash reports, or diagnostic information to help improve app performance. This data is handled according to Apple’s privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

FirePumpSim is intended for firefighter and driver/operator training. The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Training Disclaimer

FirePumpSim is for education, practice, and reference only. It does not replace department SOPs, certified instruction, manufacturer guidance, or real-world fireground decision-making.
